The process

Three steps between you and a decision you can defend.

What FarmMind AI does

One field profile. Six ways to see it clearly.

Match scoring

Every crop gets a transparent percentage — broken down by soil, season, rainfall, temperature, pH, water, and nitrogen fit.

Risk radar

Flags conditions sitting near the edge of a crop's tolerance — before a dry spell or cold snap turns into lost yield.

Profit math

Gross revenue, input cost, net return, and ROI — calculated for your exact farm size, not a generic per-acre average.

Rotation planner

A 3-season sequence built around your top crop — pairing in nitrogen-fixers where your soil needs them most.

Soil memory

Simulates 4 seasons ahead — tracking pH drift, nitrogen depletion, and overall soil health under your chosen strategy.

What-if lab

Test irrigation upgrades, better nitrogen, or a wetter season — and see exactly which crops move, and by how much.

What you get back

A crop decision you can actually defend.
